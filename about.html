W

How I work

  • Start with the system
    I map the architecture, actors, and flows before I write a single sentence.
  • Clarify the audience
    Every artifact has a primary reader and a decision or action it supports.
  • Ask adversarial questions
    What breaks? Who’s accountable? What would a skeptical reviewer challenge?
  • Design the container
    Templates, sections, and patterns come first—then content fills the structure.
  • Write for reuse
    I look for patterns that can become standards, not one-off documents.
  • Collaborate with SMEs
    I treat interviews as joint problem-solving, not transcription.
  • Iterate with intent
    Each revision has a purpose: clarity, alignment, or decision-readiness.
  • Measure impact
    I care whether documentation changes behavior, reduces risk, or accelerates delivery.
