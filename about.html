📘About Melissa Berthold

I am a senior technical writer focused on enterprise IT, infrastructure, and SaaS platforms. My work centers on making complex technical information clear, accurate, and usable for engineering, operations, and customer-facing audiences. I bridge architecture, implementation, and day-to-day operations through documentation that is structured, consistent, and aligned with how teams actually work.

⚙️Core strengths

  • Architecture and infrastructure documentation
  • Customer-facing technical documentation and proposals
  • Documentation lifecycle ownership from intake through versioning
  • Knowledge base articles and user-focused content
  • Release notes, deployment guides, and implementation guides
  • Repository governance, taxonomy, and content structure
  • SaaS platform and network environment documentation
  • Statement of work aligned deliverables

🧩How I work

  • Align documentation with Jira and Azure DevOps workflows
  • Apply structured authoring, templates, and reusable components
  • Collaborate closely with architects, engineers, product, and operations
  • Maintain audit-ready, consistent, and clear documentation standards
  • Prioritize accuracy through SME validation and targeted questions
  • Support Agile and SAFe delivery and CI/CD release cycles
